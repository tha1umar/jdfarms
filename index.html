About

JD · City, Country

Rooted in the land, grown through experience.

ELS Green Gold Agro Allied Limited is a diversified agro-allied company established to provide sustainable agricultural production, agribusiness solutions, agricultural consultancy, and operational support services across Nigeria. Since inception, the company has focused on combining innovation, modern agricultural techniques, and professional management systems to improve productivity and profitability within the agricultural value chain.

The company operates in crop production, greenhouse farming, poultry production, fishery operations, farm establishment, agricultural logistics, and consultancy services. Our approach combines practical field experience with modern agronomic practices to ensure efficient operations.
